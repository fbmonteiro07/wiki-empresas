### Figure 1: 2026 Capacity Expansions Concentrate on HBM (TrendForce slide, marked "FOR StoneX USE ONLY")
Bullets:
- Competition for HBM market share intensified during 2025. In the current year of 2026, HBM4 is the key driver.
- Global DRAM production capacity is projected to climb from around 1,966K at the end of 2025 to 2,181K at the end of 2026.
- Measured against total global DRAM wafer production capacity, TSV processing capacity is projected to increase from approximately 19% in late 2025 to 23% by the end of 2026.
- CXMT leads in DRAM production capacity expansion, but its presence in the HBM market will remain limited due to R&D challenges.

### Figure 2: HBM3e Prices Climb Higher Amid Supply Shortages (TrendForce slide, marked "FOR StoneX USE ONLY"), under section "HBM pricing"
Bullets:
- HBM3e demand maintained strong upward momentum from mid-2025 through early 2026. As a result, contract prices have settled within a wide range, varying based on the timing of negotiations.
- Although the upward revision in shipment volume is relatively modest, significant price hikes — along with the incremental contributions from increasing HBM4 penetration — have resulted in a slight upward adjustment to the blended ASP (blended average contract price) of HBM products, now projected at +2% YoY.
- Buyers and suppliers are currently negotiating 2027 HBM contracts, and both HBM3e and HBM4 contract prices could see further increases compared to 2026 levels.
