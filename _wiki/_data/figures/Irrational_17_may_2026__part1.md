## p.15 — Google AI Overview screenshot: "why 3 phase ac"

Screenshot of a Google search AI Overview. Key text: three-phase AC is the standard for power generation/transmission because it "delivers constant, efficient power with less conductor material than single-phase systems". Key Reasons listed: Constant Power Delivery; Material Efficiency (Cost-Effective); Smoother Torque for Motors; High Power Density ("3-phase systems can deliver nearly twice the power of single-phase systems without doubling the number of wires, making them ideal for high-density environments like data centers, according to Vertiv"); Optimal Balance ("sweet spot"). Comparison with Single-Phase: single-phase used for residential lighting and small appliances (120V/230V); 3-phase essential for higher power needs, three distinct AC signals 120 degrees apart, neutral wire often removable in balanced loads. Cited sources within screenshot: YouTube-ElectroBOOM, Reddit, Quora, Vertiv, Wikipedia.

Caption below: "Probing the nodes between each pair of transistors, you can see the switching action. How the transistors turn on and off to generate a square wave."

Source line: Google search screenshot (AI Overview).
