## p.22 — "4 SOC 8 HBM template" and "4 SOC 16 HBM template" (package layout diagrams)

Two schematic package-layout diagrams. Source line printed under the first diagram: "Source: SemiAnalysis". No numeric data series or axes — architectural diagrams only.

**Diagram 1 — 4 SOC 8 HBM template:** 2x2 grid of four XPU dies; 8 HBM4 stacks (each labeled "HBM4" with "HBM Base Die") placed 2 per XPU on the outer left/right edges; "D2D PHY" interfaces between adjacent XPUs; "I/O" / D2D PHY strips along top and bottom edges. SemiAnalysis watermark over lower-right XPU.

**Body text between diagrams:** "This is a sub-optimal trade-off given AI workloads are often memory bound rather than compute bound. This is why the Rubin Ultra package is more oblong shaped with a single row of SOCs. This increases edge and allows for double the HBM sites."

**Diagram 2 — 4 SOC 16 HBM template:** single row of four XPU dies (oblong package); 16 HBM4 stacks (with HBM Base Die), 4 per XPU (2 above, 2 below); D2D PHY links between adjacent XPUs; I/O PHY strips on left and right package edges.
