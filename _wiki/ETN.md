## Snapshot
Eaton is a global power-management company supplying electrical components, switchgear, busway, and power-distribution equipment — increasingly an "AI-factory power" name as hyperscaler buildouts require grid interconnection and in-facility power distribution. ETN sits in the datacenter-electrical basket alongside [VRT](VRT.md), [GEV](GEV.md), and [PWR](PWR.md), and is routinely tagged a beneficiary whenever datacenter-capex estimates are revised higher. A 2026-06-11 Reverse Morris Trust merging ETN's Mobility Group into Dana (DAN) sharpens the company toward higher-margin Electrical/Aerospace.

## Current state (latest quarter)
Q1 2026 (reported ~2026-05-05) was characterized as mixed-to-negative: full-year margin guide trimmed and Q2 guidance below Street (Vital Knowledge, 2026-05-05) — ETN grouped with CMI on margin shortfall, vs. DD/ROK upside. **Corporate event — ETN/DAN Reverse Morris Trust (2026-06-11):** ETN's Mobility Group to merge into Dana via RMT; deal value ~$5.1B / 8.3x 2026E pro forma EBITDA (5.9x including ~$250MM run-rate synergies). ETN receives a $1.1B cash distribution; ETN holders own ≥50.1% of the combined ~$10B-EV entity. Described as "immediately accretive to Eaton's organic growth rate and operating margins upon closing." (Vital Knowledge, 2026-06-11; JPM / Rajat Gupta — DAN coverage, 2026-06-11)

## Debate / thesis
- **Bull:** AI-capex exposure is the core driver — MS Capex Tracker (2026-05-18) shows NTM capex +38% y/y (hyperscalers +126%) and named ETN among "most-supported" names (ETN, ROK, VRT, FPS, HUBB). 800V HVDC transition pulled ahead: NVDA Vera Rubin and GOOGL next-gen AI DCs adopting 800V HVDC with small-volume shipments from Q3 2026; ETN named beneficiary alongside Delta Electronics, [VRT](VRT.md), Schneider, Siemens (@aleabitoreddit, 2026-06-15). META capex (~$10B+ '26/'27) flagged positive for VRT/ETN/PWR/GEV (2026-05-18). Mobility divestiture sharpens portfolio toward higher-margin, more AI-levered Electrical/Aerospace segments.
- **Bear:** Q1 2026 print was the soft spot — full-year margin guide trimmed and Q2 below Street, which needs to reconcile with the otherwise-bullish AI-capex narrative (Vital Knowledge, 2026-05-05). Competitive pressure vs. Hubbell (HUBB), Regal Rexnord, and [VRT](VRT.md) in DC switchgear. RMT deployment of the $1.1B cash distribution (buyback vs. debt vs. M&A) undecided.
