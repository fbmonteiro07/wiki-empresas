What this is. A reconstruction of an "AI-lab ARR" view using only OpenRouter's public usage feed. It measures developer/API traction routed through OpenRouter — a real, high-frequency leading indicator, but a slice: it excludes first-party enterprise API and consumer subscriptions (ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini), which are the bulk of frontier-lab ARR. Dollars are tokens × OpenRouter list price = spend at list (an upper bound), not realized revenue.
