## Snapshot
Power Integrations designs high-voltage analog/mixed-signal ICs for power conversion, serving consumer electronics, communications, industrial, and computer end markets. POWI sits on the watchlist mainly as a derivative of the NVIDIA 800VDC datacenter-power architecture thesis — FinTwit (late May–early June 2026) names POWI as collaborating with NVDA on "main stages" of 800VDC AI-rack power distribution, alongside [NVTS](NVTS.md) and AOSL. Sell-side coverage in the ported corpus is sparse (SIG/Susquehanna post-earnings notes; UBS/MS subject-line-only mentions).

## Debate / thesis
- **Bull:** POWI is a named architectural partner in NVDA's 800VDC power stack for Kyber/Rubin Ultra racks — "POWI collaborating with NVDA on main stages of 800VDC racks" (@paradislabs, 2026-05-31/06-01). The bull case is that NVDA validation of power semis as core AI infrastructure (analogous to the $4B injection into [LITE](LITE.md)+[COHR](COHR.md) on optics) triggers a re-rating. Legacy 54V architecture is now a bottleneck; 800VDC is the structural fix.
- **Bear:** SIG/Rolland flagged "caution around consumer" (2026-02-06), consistent with the 4Q25 guidance miss — consumer end-market weakness is a real headwind. Crucially, power semis have architecture validation but **not** the capital-injection catalyst that re-rated optics — "Nvidia has done similar architectural & named-partner equivalent for power. But without the capital injection / single wake-up catalyst" (@paradislabs, 2026-06-01). The $4B NVDA optics deal remains the model to watch.
- **Where the sell-side stands:** No broker PTs surfaced in the ported corpus. SIG/Susquehanna (Christopher Rolland) is the only firm with POWI-specific post-earnings notes (no PT extracted). UBS (Robert Ruple) and MS (Tom Wigg) include POWI only in multi-ticker subject lines with no body-level commentary. In the crowdsourced 800V DC power-semis ranked list, POWI sits in the "long tail" with [NVTS](NVTS.md) and AOSL, behind IFNNY (~$116B), [ON](ON.md) (~$46B), Lite-On, VICR, [WOLF](WOLF.md) (@aleabitoreddit, 2026-06-09).

## Catalysts / what to watch
- Whether NVDA makes/signals a **direct capital investment** in POWI (the LITE+COHR optics-deal analog).
- Which specific NVDA rack programs POWI is designed into (Kyber, Rubin Ultra) and the revenue opportunity per rack.
- Next print: revenue, gross margin, guidance, and the magnitude of the 4Q25 miss.
- Datacenter vs. consumer/industrial mix shift.

## Risks
- Consumer end-market weakness (SIG caution theme).

- Lack of a concrete NVDA anchor investment vs. the optics complex.
